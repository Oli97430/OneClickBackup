Capabilities

Everything you need
for your disks.

From quick backups to full system clones, partition recovery to SMART monitoring. One tool, zero dependencies.

💾

Backup & Restore

Full disk, partition, system, and incremental backups. ZIP compression, AES-256 encryption, cloud sync.

📋

Disk Cloning

Full disk clone, OS-only migration, and network cloning via UNC paths. Resize-to-fit for smaller targets.

🔍

Partition Recovery

Quick and deep scan modes with filesystem signature detection. Recover lost NTFS, FAT32, and exFAT partitions.

📊

SMART Monitoring

Full S.M.A.R.T. attribute parsing, disk benchmarks, surface tests, and HTML health reports.

🛡️

Secure Wipe

DoD 5220.22-M multi-pass erasure with verification. Quick wipe and secure 7-pass modes.

⏰

Scheduling

Daily, weekly, and monthly backup scheduling via Windows Task Scheduler. Full operation history.

💿

Disk Imaging

Create, mount, and convert VHD, VHDX, and raw IMG disk images.

🔧

Partition Tools

Create, resize, merge, format, delete partitions. MBR/GPT conversion. NTFS/FAT32 conversion.

🌐

5 Languages

English, French, Spanish, German, and Arabic with full RTL support. Hot-switch at runtime.
